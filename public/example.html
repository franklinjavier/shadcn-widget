How to Install

Installing the chat widget on your website is as simple as adding this single line of code:

<script src="https://your-domain.com/embed.js"></script>

Note: The widget now automatically loads the necessary CSS, ensuring all styles are applied correctly when embedded in any site.

Place it just before the closing </body> tag in your HTML, and the widget will automatically appear on your site.
